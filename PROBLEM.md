# TOP 10 PROBLEMS

1. **Automotive** - Why can't first time used car buyers verify car histories, conditions and future maintenance costs?

2. **Housing** - Why can't renters access verified mold, pests or hazards before signing leases?

3. **Healthcare** - Why is caring for aging parents for sole earners across cities still fragmented & stressful?

4. **Healthcare** - Why do teachers lack real time mental health support at work?

5. **Career** - Why do STEM students face education loan anxiety from unclear costs in 2026?

6. **Consumer Services** - Why can't consumers see verified kitchen safety standards on food delivery apps?

7. **Hardware** - Why is connecting specialized hardware to laptops still unreliable and frustrating?

8. **Healthcare** - Why is distinguishing sprains from fractures impossible without costly hospital visits?

9. **Career** - Why do people leaving formal education lack structure, mentorship and clear career guidance?

10. **Healthcare** - Why can't anxious people find trustworthy non-prescription sleep solutions easily?
